# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone. Be respectful,
assume good intent, focus criticism on the work, and protect private or regulated information.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, publishing private information, or
misusing repository access. Report incidents privately to the IXO World maintainers through the contact
route in `SECURITY.md`. Maintainers may remove content or participation that violates these expectations.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
